# iPhone Asset Research Plan

Question: find usable real iPhone device assets/images for website product display.

Subtopics:
1. Official Apple marketing/identity resources: discover whether downloadable transparent product assets exist and usage constraints.
2. Reliable third-party mockup/asset sources: find PNG/PSD/SVG assets with acceptable licensing for web mockup use.
3. Implementation path: select assets downloadable without account friction, store locally, credit if needed.

Synthesis: choose safest available assets, avoid Apple logos/branding misuse, replace generated SVG placeholders with local real-looking device images.
